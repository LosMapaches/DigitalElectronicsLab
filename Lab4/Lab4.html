Lab 4: Logic Gates

An inveter with a capacitive load, a ring oscillator, a NAND gate, and a NOR gate were built. The inverterter with another transistor as a capacitive load was used to measture tPHL and tPLH. The load transistor was used for time measurements because the propagation delay would be the same between two transistors in a ring oscillator. This allows for the use of f=1/n*(tPHL+tPLH) for estimating the frequency of a 3-ring oscillator.  A CD4007 chip was used. tPLH was found to be 20.0 ns and tPHL was found to be 16.4 ns. This gives a theoretical frequency of 9.16 MHz. The following is the experimental measurements of tPHL and tPLH from the oscilloscope for the inverter:
